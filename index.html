Before You Begin

🗺️
What is Timeless Pins?
Your relationship on a map

Timeless Pins is a private, interactive map made just for us. Every place we've been together, every song that reminds us of each other, every route we've walked — it all lives here, beautifully pinned onto the world.

📍 Love Coordinates ⭐ Love Trails 💗 Sweet Spots 🧸 Snuggle Spots 🗺️ Journey Map 🌿 Memory Garden 🖼️ Pin Board
📱
Opening the Map
Getting started
  • 1Open timeless-pins.vercel.app in your phone browser or on a computer.
  • 2Wait for the pink loading screen to fade — your memories will appear as pins on the map automatically.
  • 3The map will auto-zoom to fit all existing pins so you see everything at once.
  • 4Use two fingers to zoom, or pinch on mobile. Drag to pan around the map.
💡The toolbar at the top has all the buttons you need. On mobile, scroll the toolbar sideways if buttons are cut off.

Love Coordinates 📍

📍
Memory Pins
The heart of the map

Love Coordinates are our main memory markers — the places that meant something. Each one can hold a name, a date, photos, and a description. They appear as beautiful rose-coloured pins on the map.

  • 1Tap the 📍 button in the top toolbar. The cursor changes to a crosshair.
  • 2Tap anywhere on the map where you want to place the memory. A form will slide up.
  • 3Fill in the Memory Name (required), Location, Date, and Description.
  • 4Tap the photo area to add photos — you can add as many as you like!
  • 5Tap Save Memory 💕 and watch your pin appear on the map.
🔍Zoom in close (zoom level 14+) and the pin will show its name label floating below it.
👆
Viewing a Memory
Reliving the moment
  • 1Tap any rose-coloured pin on the map to open its detail panel.
  • 2Scroll through the photos — tap any photo to open it full screen.
  • 3In full screen, use the ‹ › arrows flanking the title to browse all photos from that memory.
  • 4Tap the ✕ or tap outside the panel to close it.
2

Love Trails ⭐

⭐
Our Journeys
Draw paths we've walked together

Love Trails let you draw any route or path on the map — the walk to the café, the road trip route, the way home. You draw it yourself, tap by tap, exactly how you remember it.

  • 1Tap the ⭐ button in the toolbar. A drawer slides up from the bottom.
  • 2Enter a Trail Name and optional description. Pick a colour for the trail.
  • 3Tap "Draw on Map 🗺️" — the drawer closes and a status bar appears at the bottom.
  • 4Tap the map point by point to trace your path. Each tap adds a dot and extends the line.
  • 5Made a mistake? Tap ↩ Undo to remove the last point.
  • 6When you're happy with the path, tap Save Trail ⭐.
✨Trails are hidden by default. Tap the ✨ Sparkles button in the toolbar to make trail stars appear on the map. Then tap a star to show/hide that trail's path.

Sweet Spots 💗

💗
Special Places
Quick meaningful markers

Sweet Spots are simple heart markers for places that are special — no photos needed, just a name and maybe a small note. They float on the map as animated pink hearts.

  • 1Tap the 💗 button in the toolbar.
  • 2Tap the map where you want to place the heart.
  • 3Enter a Title (required) and optional description.
  • 4Tap Pin It 💗 to save it.
💌Sweet Spots are perfect for "this is where we had our first date" or "this coffee shop is our spot" — quick and sentimental.
4

Snuggle Spots 🧸

🧸
Music + Memories
Places with a soundtrack

Snuggle Spots are memory pins linked to a song. Add photos, and the first one becomes the song cover art. Include a Spotify or YouTube link, and a play button appears over the cover photo.

  • 1Tap the 🧸 button in the toolbar and tap the map location.
  • 2Enter the Place Name (required).
  • 3Add photos — the first photo will be used as the song cover art, so pick something beautiful!
  • 4Paste a Song Link (Spotify, YouTube, etc.) — this is optional but makes it magical.
  • 5Tap Save Spot 🧸.
🎵When viewing a Snuggle Spot, tap the link button on the cover photo to open the song directly.
Every place has a story. Every story deserves a pin.

The map is yours to fill — one memory at a time 🌸

5

Searching for a Place 🔍

🔍
The Search Bar
Find any place in the world

The search bar at the top of the map lets you find any city, landmark, restaurant or street in the world — then add a pin right there.

  • 1Tap the search bar and type any place name.
  • 2Select a result — the map will fly to that location and mark it with a temporary circle.
  • 3A small prompt appears: "Would you like to add a pin here?"
  • 4Choose which type of pin to add — or tap "Not now" to just explore.
6

Layers & Overlays 🗺️🌿✨

These three magical toggles in the toolbar add extra layers of meaning to the map. Each one is a different way to see our story.

🗺️
Journey Map
Our timeline

Connects all Love Coordinates that have dates, in chronological order, with a dashed red line — showing the story of our relationship as a path through time.

  • →Tap 🗺️ Journey in the toolbar to toggle it on or off.
📅Works only when pins have a date saved.
🌿
Memory Garden
Our favourite areas

Draws soft pink circles around areas where we have multiple Love Coordinates close together — showing where our most cherished places cluster.

  • →Tap 🌿 Garden in the toolbar to toggle it on or off.
🌸Zoom into the map first for tighter, more precise clusters.
✨
Sparkles — Trail Stars
Reveal love trails

Trail stars are invisible by default so they don't clutter the map. Tap Sparkles to reveal all trail stars, then tap any star to show or hide that specific trail's path.

  • 1Tap ✨ Sparkles in the toolbar — all trail stars appear on the map.
  • 2Tap any ⭐ star to toggle that trail's path on and off.
  • 3Tap ✨ Sparkles again to hide all stars and all paths.
· · · · · ·
7

Memory List (PinDom) 📋

📋
All Our Memories in a List
Navigate to any pin instantly

PinDom is a sidebar list of every single pin on the map — searchable by type, with instant fly-to navigation.

  • 1Tap the 📋 button in the toolbar to open the Memory List sidebar.
  • 2Use the filter pills at the top (All / Love / Sweet / Snuggle / Trail) to filter by type.
  • 3Tap any item in the list — the map will smoothly fly to that pin's location.
  • 4Tap the 📋 button again or tap outside to close the sidebar.
8

Our Pin Board 🖼️

🖼️
Pinterest-Style Photo Gallery
Every photo, beautifully displayed

The Pin Board collects every photo from every Love Coordinate and displays them in a beautiful Pinterest-style masonry layout — different heights, natural flow, gorgeous to scroll through.

  • 1Tap the 🖼️ button in the toolbar to open the Pin Board.
  • 2Scroll through your photos — they arrange themselves in a natural, staggered layout.
  • 3Tap any photo to open it full screen in the lightbox.
  • 4In the lightbox, use the ‹ name › arrows to browse more photos, and see 1/3 style counters.
  • 5Tap "📍 Take me to the Love Coordinate" to fly back to that pin on the map.
✈️This is the best way to relive memories visually — just sit and scroll through our photos together.
